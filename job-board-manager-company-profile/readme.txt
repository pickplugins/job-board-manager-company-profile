	Company Profile for Job Board Manager plugin

== Description ==

Display available job from any company and display company information via single page.
and display ajax list of predefined company list on job editing. 

### Job Board Manager - Company Profile by [http://pickplugins.com](http://pickplugins.com)

* [Live Demo](http://www.pickplugins.com/demo/job-board-manager/company/microsoft/)
* [Buy Premium](http://www.pickplugins.com/item/job-board-manager-company-profile/)
* [documentation](https://pickplugins.com/docs/documentation/job-board-manager-company-profile/)
* [Get Job Board Manager &raquo;](https://wordpress.org/plugins/job-board-manager/)


<strong>Plugin Features</strong>

* Awesome company profile page.
* Ajax list of companies on job submission. company fields info will fill automatically if company listed available.
* Company cover image, Logo, City, Descriptions, Address, Website, Founded, Type, Size, Revenue, Mission text.
* Available job list form company.
* Company follow features. job form company will display on job feed.

<strong>Premium Features</strong>

* Company listing request form.
* Related company list at bottom of company single page.
* Company list with pagination.
* Star reviews for companies


<strong>Translation</strong>

Plugin is translation ready, you can contribute in translation. please contact with us http://pickplugins.com/contact/

== Installation ==

1. Install as regular WordPress plugin.<br />
2. Go your plugin setting via WordPress Dashboard and find "<strong>Job Board Manager - Company Profile</strong>" activate it.<br />
